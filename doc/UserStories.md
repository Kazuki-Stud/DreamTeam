### Domain Description

The product is a **digital lost and found platform** that automates the matching of lost items with found ones. 

* **What it does**: It acts as database where users reports of missing or discovered belongings. The system uses a matching engine to connect owners and finders based on keywords, locations, and item attributes.
* **Problem it solves**: 
    * Eliminates the need to manually scroll through social media posts or local group chats to find a specific item.
    * Stops "fake finders" from seeing sensitive details used to prove ownership, preventing extortion or reward fraud.
    * Users can report items without immediately exposing their phone number or home address to the entire internet.
    * Alerts ensure owners don't miss a find just because they weren't checking the app at that exact moment.
    * Organizations can stop managing physical logs and answering constant phone inquiries by using an automated, self-service database for their visitors.

---

### Target Audience

#### 1. Property Owners & Finders
* **Profile**: Individuals who have lost valuables. Passersby or citizens who find items and want to return them ethically.
* **Needs**: 
    * Organized way to search for their item.
    * Real-time notifications so they don't miss a find.
    * Security measures to verify a finder is legitimate before sharing personal info.
    * A simple, low-friction way to report a find without legal risks.
    * Clear instructions on how to handle found property (legal guidance).
    * Anonymity until a match is confirmed to avoid harassment.

#### 2. Partners: Lost and Found Bureaus
* **Profile**: Professional agencies and existing city or regional databases.
* **Needs**: 
    * **Database Synchronization**: Ability to exchange records to increase the global pool of potential matches.
    * **API Access**: Standardized integration points to pull/push data between systems.

#### 3. Communities & Organizations
* **Profile**: Large venues or local community groups.
* **Needs**: 
    * A centralized hub to direct people when items are reported.
    * Reduced administrative load in managing physical lost-and-found bins.
